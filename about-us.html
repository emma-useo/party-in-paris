The little story

Erwann, Emma et Léo, 3 students who wanted to buy their tickets for the biggest event of the year in Paris. The tickets were taken very quickly, and by the time the 3 students found the event page and the ticket office, the event was sold out. They came to the conclusion that there should be a way to find out about an event and book the ticket much more easily. So they created Party'n Paris.
